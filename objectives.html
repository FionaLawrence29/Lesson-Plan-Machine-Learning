SMART Objectives

  • Define machine learning and explain its significance within 15 minutes of instruction.
  • Differentiate between supervised and unsupervised learning models with 80% accuracy by the end of the lesson.
  • Describe a real-world application of machine learning in a group activity within 20 minutes.
  • Classify problems into ML categories (classification/regression/clustering) with 80% accuracy in a quiz.
